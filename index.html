Giving a Helping Hand
To Those in Need

At The Golden Hands Charitable Trust, we are dedicated to transforming lives through:

  • 🐾 Animal Welfare – feeding, rescuing, and medicating stray animals.
  • 🌳 Tree Plantation – restoring green spaces and combating climate change.
  • 🌊 Nature Cleanup – cleaning rivers, parks, and public areas for a sustainable tomorrow.
  • 📚 Educating the Underprivileged – spreading happiness through learning and emotional support.
  • 🩺 Health & Hygiene Drives – organizing free medical camps and awareness programs.
  • 💼 Skill Training – empowering youth and women with vocational training.
  • 🛖 Shelter Support – distributing essentials to the homeless and seasonal migrants.
  • ♻️ Sustainable Living – promoting eco-friendly habits and responsible living.
