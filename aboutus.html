What We Do

Animal Welfare

We feed, medicate, rescue, and protect stray animals, creating a compassionate ecosystem for all creatures.

Green Earth

We plant trees and clean parks/rivers to ensure a greener, more breathable environment for future generations.

Spreading Happiness

We spend time with underprivileged children and elderly, spreading joy and offering informal education support.

Sanitation & Health

From providing clean drinking water to hygiene awareness and basic medical care, we uplift the most vulnerable.
