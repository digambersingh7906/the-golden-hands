About Us

The Golden Hands Charitable Trust, founded by Digamber Singh, Shubham Uniyal, and Shubham Khatri on 14th December 2022, is a registered nonprofit organization headquartered at Saraswati Bhawan, Defence Colony, Ward No-8, Najibabad Road, Kotdwara, Uttarakhand 246149. Our trust is legally established through a formal Deed of Trust and is committed to promoting social welfare and sustainability across India.

The Trust was initiated with a humble sum of â‚¹3,300 and is founded on the values of empathy, service, and sustainable development. Our mission is to bring positive change in the lives of the underprivileged, protect nature, and empower communities.

1. Animal Welfare

We feed and medicate stray animals, protect cattle harmed on roads, build shelters, and promote kind treatment of all animals. Our goal is to create a harmonious relationship between humans and animals.

2. Planting Trees & Environment

Through tree plantation, agroforestry, and cleanliness drives, we work to ensure ecological balance, clean water, and sustainable resources for the future.

3. Spreading Happiness & Education

We teach poor children, promote vocational skills among the differently abled, and support women and elderly education to enhance livelihoods.

4. Sanitation & Preventive Healthcare

We eradicate hunger, promote sanitation, and ensure access to clean drinking water and basic medical care for the poor, blind, and disabled.

We also support armed forces veterans, run medical and educational centers, and welcome collaboration with like-minded NGOs. The Trust is governed by a Board of Trustees who ensure proper financial accountability and transparency, meeting regularly and maintaining audited accounts.
